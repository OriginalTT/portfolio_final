ABOUT

A motivated web developer/ videographer with experience creating websites for small companies and videos for numerous projects. Knowledgeable in user experience design, prototyping, and developing processes. He also has experience in filming, editing, directing, and motion graphic design. Able to effectively self-manage during independent projects and collaborate in a team setting. Native Japanese speaker with proficient English skills. He was born in Tokyo, Japan, and spent half his life there and another half in Toronto, Canada.

WORKS

Let's Drink Some Coffee!

Motion Design Project

Designed motion graphics that emphasizes on call to action through the effective use of ethos, pathos, and logos. This project was for one of the university courses. It was required to be in a vertical orientation and under thirty seconds. The topic of coffee was chosen as many people misunderstand the effect of coffee, thus causing unnecessary worry about the consumption of coffee.
